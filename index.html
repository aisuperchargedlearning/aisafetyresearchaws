AIsafetyresearch Mission statement

AIsafetyresearch, a non-profit initiative supported by small enterprise technologists including  A7technology Inc., is dedicated to pioneering and disseminating research in the critical field of Artificial Intelligence. Our mission is to advance knowledge and innovation, focusing on areas such as AI Safety, Educational Applications of AI, and AI's role in the Medical Field. Committed to fostering a deeper understanding and responsible governance of AI, we actively support policymakers and government agencies with insightful research and guidance. Building on extensive experience in developing high-density secure compute architectures and global communication solutions, AIsafetyresearch strives to shape a future where AI is used responsibly and effectively for societal betterment. Our vision is a world where the transformative power of AI is harnessed for safety, education, and health, benefiting all of humanity.
